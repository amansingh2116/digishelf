Digishelf Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 19, 2025

Welcome to Digishelf. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information you provide when creating an account, purchasing books, or contacting us. This includes names, email addresses, payment details, and reading habits. We also automatically collect usage data, device information, and browsing patterns using cookies and similar technologies.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to process purchases, deliver content, personalize recommendations, maintain our service, communicate with you, and comply with legal obligations.

3. Information Sharing

We may share information with service providers who help operate our platform, process payments, and analyze data. We share anonymized reading statistics with publishers and authors. We'll disclose information when legally required to do so.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your information, though no method of electronic transmission is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict the processing of your data. Contact us to exercise these rights.

6. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

7. International Transfers

Your information may be transferred to countries with different data protection laws than your own.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this policy periodically. Continued use of our service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
